Study Technique

I like to study but I realize my big mistake is not staying consistent so i rely on my short-term memory for exam preparation. So I understand the fears of not being able to recollect subjects after the exam is over. Now I am changing it.

  • Retrospective Revision: Blank piece of paper, write down whatever you know about the subject
  • Active Recall: Just Recollect any topics
  • Spaced Repetition: Because my intention is to have a long term memory retention, i need to frequently recollect the subjects I have already studied.
  • Solve as many sums as I can get my hands-on: Since this is an exam I need to do this diligently.
  • Timeboxing, using calender
  • Most of my time should be spent analysing solved questions No-1 Rule
  • Animedoro Rocks!
  • Weekdays-Study & Weekends-Revise & Solve
  • When Tired, Practise Yoga Nidra + eyemasks
  • ParetoConcept - "It's a little hard wrap our heads around this concept, but it works"
  • Write as many exams as you can get your hands on
  • Stress
  • Anxious
  • I have lived happy without the exam, i can live normal without having attempted the exam. The point being: just learn everyday. That is really all you need done NOW
